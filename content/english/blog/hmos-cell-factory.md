Challenges in Building HMOs Cell Factories: Low substrate specificity of glycosyltransferases, difficult co-regulation of precursor synthesis pathways, unclear key regulatory targets.

## Design-Build-Test-Learn (DBTL) Iterative Framework for Enzyme Molecular Modification

An integrated DBTL iterative framework incorporating molecular dynamics simulations, quantum mechanics calculations, and structural biology analysis to enhance enzyme substrate specificity and catalytic activity.

## Multi-Target Dual Base Editing (MultiduBE)

Using CRISPR/dCas12a to build a Multiplex Dual Base Editor enabling targeted in-situ mutagenesis of specific genes for enzyme mutant library construction.

## Multi-Scale Integrated Metabolic Network Model: etiBsu1209

Leveraging Model-Tool (automated model construction software) to develop a multi-scale integrated metabolic network incorporating enzyme constraints, thermodynamic constraints, and transcriptional regulatory networks. Accuracy: 74.3%.

## Machine Learning-Enhanced Multi-Omics Model

Using high-quality multi-omics datasets to construct 34 machine learning-based ensemble models (iBsu1209-ME). Accuracy: 87.9%.

## Temperature-Sensitive Gene Circuits

Engineered temperature-sensitive transcription regulatory elements based on CI857 transcription factor, with single-input multi-output and bidirectional gene circuits.

## Programmable Multifunctional Biomolecular Condensates

Using liquid-liquid phase separation mechanisms to design artificial disordered proteins (SIDPs) through a building block strategy.

## Cascade Gene Circuit-Based High-Throughput Screening

Cascade gene circuits for metabolite-fluorescence signal amplification and fine-tuned metabolic pathway regulation; crRNA library with 5184 optimized combinations; flow sorting-automated picking for high-yield strains.

## 2'-Fucosyllactose (2'-FL) Cell Factory Optimization

Through endogenous GDP-fucose pathway assembly, transporter engineering, and precursor supply strategies, achieving de novo synthesis of 2'-FL with lactose/sucrose as substrates. 2'-FL + 3-FL titer reached 30.1 g/L (glycerol as sole carbon source).
